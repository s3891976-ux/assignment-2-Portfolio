Yokai Delusion

2D ANIMATION • 2024

The story follows a young and curious Tanuki who sneaks into a house where the owner of that place owns a small field of strange mushrooms, the urge of wanting to taste that mushroom rise inside the Tanuki so he risks to steal them all. After that, when he gets a lot of mushrooms and goes back to the forest, he quickly hallucinates after tasting a mushroom. From seeing mountains of gold to a lake filled with Sake wine which is all due to the effect of the mushroom that caused it. In reality, he was eating rock, swimming in a pile of leaves and drinking mud wildly, leaving him there to keep seeing things until the toxin in the mushroom ran out

LOST

DIGITAL VIDEO • 2025

"Lost" is project in Digital Media, explores a bright yet empty digital world that slowly transforms into a nightmare, reflecting the feeling of being chased, trapped, and losing oneself with no clear way out.
