ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Bao Chau, and I am currently a Digital Media student at RMIT University. I used to blend my academic background in international business with a deep passion for art and animation. From a young age, I have enjoyed sketching and storytelling, often bringing my imaginative worlds to life through various mediums such as colored pencils, watercolor, line art, and, more recently, digital art.

Although my academic journey initially led me toward global trade, my artistic pursuits have remained a constant source of inspiration and creativity. Today, I continue to chase my dream by exploring new experiences and possibilities within the Digital Media major.
